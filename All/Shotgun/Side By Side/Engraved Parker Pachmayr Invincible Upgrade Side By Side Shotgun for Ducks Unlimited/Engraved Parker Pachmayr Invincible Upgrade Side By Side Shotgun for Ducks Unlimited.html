Description Manufactured in 1928 by Parker Gun Company of Meriden, Connecticut. Upgraded by Frank Pachmayr at unknown date for donation to Ducks Unlimited. Blued 30" SXS Parker fluid steel barrels with matted Parker addressed rib with "Invincible By Pachmayr" in gold. Barrels have Doll's head rib extension, twin bead sights, fixed full and full chokes, 2-3/4" chambers, and ejectors with Parker overload proof markings. Muzzle of barrels have banded gold line border with scroll design in gold. Breech has deep relief floral engraving with scroll gold work. Left breech has a pheasant in flight and Fleur de Lis, all in gold inlay. Right breech has a duck in flight with Fleur de Lis, all in gold inlay. Polished steel "No. 1" frame marked "INVINCIBLE" with side clips and shaped barrel lugs. Frame is engraved in "INVINCIBLE" pattern with right panel having gold pheasant in flight and six radiant gold leaves surrounding six silver inlayed flowers. Left panel is engraved with gold duck in flight and six radiant gold leaves surrounding six silver inlayed flowers. Floor plate has "Parker Bros" in gold by welded hinge pin with gold fanned tail strutting quail surrounded by gold scroll. Action has automatic safety and double triggers with pierced trigger guard bow and opening lever. Feather crotch walnut pistol grip stock with flame checkered wrist and checkered side panels with Fleur di Lis carving behind side panels. Stock is fitted with Pachmayr "Old English" solid rubber recoil pad with black Pachmayr grip cap and brass plaque inscribed "DONATED BY NAN AND FRANK PACHMAYR TO DUCKS UNLIMITED". Matching checkered splinter forearm with scroll engraved case hardened hardware. LOP: 14-1/2". DAC: 1-1/2". DAH: 2-3/8". Weight: 7lbs. 2oz. CONDITION: 95% overall condition with some inlays starting to dull. Wood has some dings and marks from storage and handling. The wood appears to be manufactured proud of lower tang. Mirror bores and good mechanics. CALIBER: 16 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic. S/N: 225502.
