Description The Cody Firearms Records Office confirms this was manufactured in 1948 by Winchester Repeating Arms. Upgraded by Frank Pachmayr at an unknown date and engraved by master engraver Angelo Bee. Blued 26" SXS Winchester proof steel barrels, matted rib with Lyman bead front sight and steel bead mid sight, marked in gold "CUSTOM BY PACHMAYR". Barrel choked WS-1 and WS-2 with ejectors and 2-3/4" chambers. Barrels have deep relief engraving at muzzle and breech with gold line banding . Right breech is also decorated with gold duck in flight surrounded by gold scroll line border. Left breech is decorated with gold grouse in flight surrounded by gold scroll line border. Polished grey steel receiver with extended side panel featuring floral, marsh, and wood scene engravings. Left side has four gold ducks in flight in marsh scene with semi circle gold line border signed "AB". Right side has four gold grouse inlays in various stages of flight in wooded scene with gold semi circle line border. Floor plate has seven gold quail against field scene with "SKEET" in gold in front of trigger guard. Gun is fitted with scalloped grey steel trigger guard featuring two gold quail on trigger bow. Action has standard Winchester gold platted single selective gold trigger with non selective safety. Walnut feather crotch pistol grip stock with flame Fleur de Lis checkering. Fitted with Pachmayr "Old English" solid rubber recoil pad and Pachmayr hard rubber grip cap, missing Pachmayr presentation inlay. Matching Fleur de Lis checkered beavertail forearm with deep relief floral engraved roller latch and forearm iron. Includes a research letter from the Cody Firearms Records Office. LOP: 14-1/2". DAC: 1-1/2". DAH: 2". Weight: 7lbs. 6oz. CONDITION: 98% barrel blue with assembly marks. Action finish starting to dull with gold starting to tarnish. 98% varnish with sharp checkering and small handling marks. Excellent bore and mechanics. CALIBER: 12 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic.
