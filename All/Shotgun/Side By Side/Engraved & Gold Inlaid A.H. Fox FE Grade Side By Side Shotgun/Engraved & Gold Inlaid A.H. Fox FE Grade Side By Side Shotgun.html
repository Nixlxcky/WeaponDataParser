Description Serial # 14179 was manufactured in 1909 by A.H. Fox Gun Company. Factory card states this gun originally had 32" barrels, 3" chambers and a straight grip buttstock. There is no record of this gun being sent back for repair. Gun currently has 28" SXS blued krupp steel barrels with matted rib, bone bead front sight with Full and Modified chokes, and 2-3/4" chambers. Right barrel is marked "KRUPP FLUID STEEL MADE BY ". Left barrel is marked "A.H. FOX GUN CO. PHILA. PA. USA". Barrels are deep scroll relief engraved at breech with typical FE grade gold lightning bolt inlays and gold band on rib at breech. Barrel flats are marked "F" with serial number and 12 gauge with Fox proof. Barrels are fitted with ejectors, both serial numbered to gun. Case hardened floral and scroll full coverage engraved action with gold pointing dog on left panel and sunburst in gold on hinge pin. Right side panel features gold pointing dog with sunburst on hinge pin. Floor plate features two gold quail in forest scene with floor plate screw featuring gold compass border inlay. Case hardened floral engraved trigger guard with serial #14179 in gold. Action watertable has scroll engraved border with "Fox Patents", serial number, and "F" marking. Top of receiver has barrel lugs with full coverage scroll engraving and gold sunburst design on opening lever pin. Gun has automatic safety with word "SAFE" in gold. Action features a Kautzky single selective trigger with left and right letters in gold. Gun is stocked in American walnut with round knob pistol grip and checkered side panels in Fleur de Lis pattern. Matching serial numbered full checkered splinter forearm with Fleur de Lis pattern, ebony diamond tip inlay and Anson & Deeley release latch. Gun has 13-5/8" pull to original style hard rubber serrated buttplate. DAC: 1-5/8". DAH: 2-3/4". Weight: 7lbs. 2oz. Includes a research letter from the Cody Firearms Records Office. CONDITION: 98% overall condition as professionally refinished and embellished. Gun has small handling marks with some light chipping on wood behind tang. Mirror bores with tight lock up and good mechanics. CALIBER: 12 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic. S/N: 14179.
