Gun has blued 32" Winchester Proof Steel barrels choked Improved Modified and Improved Modified with 2-3/4" chambers, factory vented rib and ejectors with added deep relief floral breech engraving and gold line design. Blued receiver with non factory deep floral engraving, gold embellished barrel lugs and six gold inlays including ducks in flight on right panel, dog and pheasant on left panel, and dog's head on floorplate. Gold plated single selective trigger with automatic safety. Figured custom walnut pistol grip stock with Fleur de Lis checkering similar to Winchester 21-B style, steel grip cap and Winchester hard rubber buttplate. Matching beavertail forearm with roller latch. LOP: 14-1/4". DAC: 1-1/2". DAH: 1-15/16". Weight: 8.0 lbs. Includes a research letter from the Cody Firearms Records Office.
