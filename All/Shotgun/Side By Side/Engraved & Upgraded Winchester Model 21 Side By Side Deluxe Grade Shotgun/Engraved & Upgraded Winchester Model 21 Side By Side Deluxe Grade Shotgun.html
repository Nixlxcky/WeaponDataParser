Description Manufactured in 1946 originally for Bauer Sport Shop in Seattle, Washington by Winchester Repeating Arms Company, New Haven, Connecticut. Gun was re-ordered by Abercrombie & Fitch Co., New York, NY, as a Deluxe Grade. Upgraded at unknown time by unknown professional gunsmith with 21-6 style engraving and additional six gold inlays featuring dogs and flying ducks. 30" Winchester proof SXS steel barrels with full and improved modified chokes, factory 3" chambers and matted rib, single bead front site and ejectors. Blued action with Winchester gold plated single selective trigger and automatic safety. Factory Winchester walnut pistol grip stock with diamond checkered grip, checkered side panels, factory Winchester red rubber recoil pad and Winchester logo black hard rubber grip cap. Matching factory checkered splinter forearm with roller latch. LOP: 14-3/4". DAC: 1-1/2". DAH: 2-3/8". Weight: 7lbs. 3oz. Includes a research letter from the Cody Firearms Records Office.
