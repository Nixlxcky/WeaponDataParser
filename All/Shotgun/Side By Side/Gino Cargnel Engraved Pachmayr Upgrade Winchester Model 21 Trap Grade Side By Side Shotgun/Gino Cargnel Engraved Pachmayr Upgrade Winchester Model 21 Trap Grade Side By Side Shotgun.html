Description The Cody Firearms Records Office confirms this gun was originally manufactured in 1948 by Winchester Repeating Arms as a "Trap Grade" and upgraded by Frank Pachmayr at an unknown date. Blued 30" SXS Winchester proof steel barrels with matted rib, ejectors, 2-3/4" chamber, full and full fixed chokes in 12 gauge. Barrels are embellished with deep relief floral engraving at muzzle and breech. Gold banding around muzzle and breech with a gold duck in flight on each barrel at breech. Blued deep relief engraved frame with side plate extensions. Left panel features a running dog chasing three ducks in flight, all in gold and surrounded by a gold line border. Right side features a retrieving dog with duck in mouth and two gold ducks in flight, all in gold and surrounded by a gold line border. Floor plate has five gold ducks in various stages of flight with Winchester grade marking "Trap" in gold and encircled in gold line border. Floor plate is signed with "Liege" master engraver Gino Cargnel to the left of original single selective trigger. Trigger guard is deep relief engraved with gold shield inlay and line gold border. Tang and barrel lugs are deep relief floral engraved with gold line border and fitted with non automatic safety . Blonde checkered walnut pistol grip stock done in Winchester style, Winchester red rubber recoil pad and black Winchester grip cap with German silver vanity plate. Matching Winchester style beavertail checkered forearm with roller latch and deep relief engraving and gold line work. Includes a research letter from the Cody Firearms Records Office. LOP: 14-3/8". DAC: 1-1/4". DAH: 1-3/4". Weight: 7lbs. 10oz. CONDITION: 98% overall with small handling and assembly marks. Sharp engraving and checkering with excellent bores and mechanics. CALIBER: 12 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic. S/N: 17797.
