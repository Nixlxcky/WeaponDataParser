Description Originally manufactured by Parker Gun Company under Remington Arms, circa 1937. Upgraded to "Invincible Grade" by Frank Pachmayr at unknown date and was part of his personal collection. Blued Parker 30" SXS barrels with Parker proofs. Barrels have matted rib with bead sight, Parker address and "Custom by Pachmayr" in gold. Barrels embellished with deep relief floral engraving at muzzles and breech. Barrels have Doll's head rib extension with ejectors in desirable 28 gauge and 2-3/4" chambers with full and full chokes. Case hardened "00" frame with side plate extensions, welded Parker pin with upgraded "Invincible" quality engraving and gold work. Action features scalloped barrel lugs with side clips with left side having two gold dogs watching two gold quail in flight. Right side features two dogs in gold and two quails in flight in gold. Floor plate features deep relief chiseled engraving with "Lord Of The Forest" with breath being floral engraving with five gold birds. Blued deep relief engraved trigger guard with gold dog with bird in mouth inlay and serial number in gold. Watertable has remnants of Parker markings with typical grooves for "00" frame with engine turned finish. Top of action has deep relief floral engraving with two gold floral inlays. Action is fitted with non automatic safety selector and gold plated double triggers. Gun is stocked in blonde walnut with pistol grip and Pachmayr "Old English" solid rubber buttplate. Stock is checkered in Fleur de Lis pattern with Pachmayr grip cap but missing brass Pachmayr presentation plaque. Matching walnut Fleur de Lis pattern beavertail forearm with Anson and Deeley release latch. LOP: 15". DAC: 1-5/8". DAH: 2". Weight: 6lbs. 4oz. CONDITION: 98% overall with light handling marks. Excellent bores and mechanic. CALIBER: 28 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic. S/N: 238513.
