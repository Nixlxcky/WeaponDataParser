Description Takedown gun manufactured in 1927. Standard Winchester markings on the barrel with an off set proof. The frame and barrel assembly numbers match. The barrel assembly serial appears to have been restamped. "TRAP" marked under the serial number on the frame. 30" barrel with full duck bill style vent rib and white plastic bead front sight. Nicely reapplied blued finish with custom fine scroll engraving on the barrel and frame. Left side of the frame has dogs chasing a game bird while the right shows flying ducks, all in gold inlay. Left side of the frame is signed "N. Hartliep". Fancy figured walnut stock and forearm with a nice oil finish. Horn capped pistol grip. Fine cut checkering in Fleur de Lis style pattern. Added "WINCHESTER" marked red rubber butt pad. Choke is marked "FULL". LOP is 14". CONDITION: Metal is good overall with about 85% finish remaining. Rib is loose at the action. Barrel assembly has some spots of surface rust, mainly on the right side. Bore is bright. Mechanically good. CALIBER: 12 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Curio & Relic. S/N: 507644.
