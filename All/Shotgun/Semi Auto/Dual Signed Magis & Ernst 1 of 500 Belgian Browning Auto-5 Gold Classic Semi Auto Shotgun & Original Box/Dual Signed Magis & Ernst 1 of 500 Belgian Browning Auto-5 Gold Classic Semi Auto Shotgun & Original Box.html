Description Manufactured in 1986 in Belgium by FN for Browning. Number "107" of 500. These are thought to have been sold in both the U.S. and Europe, making it a very scarce A-5. Finely finished walnut stock with sharp cut checkering on the round pistol grip. Checkering is outlined with fine oak leaf carving. Factory old style black plastic butt plate. 2-3/4" chamber.
