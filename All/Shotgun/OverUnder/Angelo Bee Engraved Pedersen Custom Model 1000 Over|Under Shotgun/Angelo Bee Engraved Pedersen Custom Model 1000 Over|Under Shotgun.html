Description Manufactured between 1973 and 1975 in Italy. Customized by Pedersen Custom Guns, owned by O.F Mossberg of North Haven, Connecticut. Blued 28" fluid steel O/U barrels with ventilated rib, single bead sight and 2-3/4" chambers. Barrels have ejectors and are fitted for screw-in chokes. Blued box lock frame with gold scroll work surrounding gold ducks on left side and gold grouse on right. Floor plate features gold scroll engraving surrounding three gold quail. Blued trigger guard with single gold quail inlay. Engraving and gold work is signed on frame by master engraver "Angelo Bee". Gun has blued single trigger with top tang barrel safety selector. Beautiful feather crotch satin finished checkered pistol grip stock with checkered side panels and fitted with "Uncle Mike's" vented recoil pad. Matching checkered Schnabel forearm with roller latch. LOP: 13-7/8". DAC: 1-5/8". DAH: 2". Weight: 7 lbs. 4oz. CONDITION: 90% barrel blue with light edge wear. 90% action wear in carry area. Original hand rubbed finish with sharp checkering and some small handling dings. Choke tubes are missing. The forearm has a repaired crack with some dislocation in finish. Crack is barely visible. Excellent bore and mechanics. CALIBER: 12 Gauge. FFL STATUS: Modern. S/N: PBA10942B.
