Description This is the earliest version with the earliest hardware of the Howe's vacuum bait in a special version frog finish. This was the first version of the frog as innovated by Howe. It dates to the introduction of the vacuum bait to the commercial market pre-1910. It is green on the top side and the bottom of the lure has striped belly paint finishes. Ultra rare with great eye appeal.
