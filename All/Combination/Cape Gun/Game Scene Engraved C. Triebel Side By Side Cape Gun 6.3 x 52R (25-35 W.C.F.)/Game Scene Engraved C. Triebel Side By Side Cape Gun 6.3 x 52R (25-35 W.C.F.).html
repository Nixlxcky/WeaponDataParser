Description Manufactured circa 1910 by C. Triebel in Augsburg, Germany. SXS 27-11/16" twist steel 16 gauge shotgun barrel beside Guss steel 6.3 X 52R (25-35 W.C.F.) with extractors. Barrel has smooth rib, rifle sights with claw scope mount bases and marked "CF Triebel Augsburg " in gold with gold barrel bands and scroll gold decoration. Unique swelled deep floral relief case hardened action with floral and game scene engraved back action locks and shaped exposed hammers. Controlled by double triggers with front being a set trigger. Tang has hidden folding peep sight. Action has horn under lever opener with steel and horn trigger guard. Figured walnut checkered stock with Bavarian cheekpiece and checkered steel butt. Stock is raised carved forest scene behind cheek with running Hirsch with Fleur de Lis checkering border. Matching checkered splinter forearm with release latch in tip. CONDITION: 50% fading barrel finish with balance turning a silvery patina. Action has sharp engraving and has faded to a silvery grey patina with no visible case colors.
