Description Circa 1915, two piece wooden gun cabinet and working skeleton key. Brass tag on top face reads: "Presented to W. Bro. James Porter P.M., PAGD of Cs, by the Brethren of St. Trillo Lodge No. 2569. A token of their esteem on the occasion of his Installation as the 21st Master of the Lodge. 1915." Top half is in good condition and the glass is free of cracks. The upper back panel appears to have been replaced. The lower piece is in good condition and features two dovetail drawers with brass hardware on the left and a storage space on the right. Holds six guns although three of the inner barrel holders on top are missing. The cabinet has general age wear throughout with slightly more on the lower piece, including a small piece of trip missing on the lower door. Top and bottom sections can be separated and are connected by dowels, a few of which are lodged or snapped in their holes. 73" H x 25" W x 12" D.
