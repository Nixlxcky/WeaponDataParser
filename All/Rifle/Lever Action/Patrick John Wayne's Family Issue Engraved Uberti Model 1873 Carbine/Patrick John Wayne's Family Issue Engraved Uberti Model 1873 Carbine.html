Description Made in Italy by Uberti as a close replica of the classic Winchester Carbine, this particular rifle was owned by Patrick Wayne, son of John Wayne, and engraved/embellished by the American Historical Society as part of their America Remembers collection. It features a custom serial number as it was issued specifically to the John Wayne Family members. 19" barrel with blade front sight and ladder rear. Factory high blue finish with the front band, rear band, lifter, trigger and hammer all in gold wash. Laser engraved side plates with a gold inlay stagecoach and cattle scene on the left. "JOHN WAYNE" banner and calvary scene in gold on the right side of the frame with his silhouette laser etched on the bottom of the elevator. Oiled wood stock and forearm with fine cut checkering on the forearm. Presents with a commemorative coin on the left side of the butt. Smooth blued steel crescent metal butt plate. Includes a signed letter from Patrick Wayne confirming his ownership of this rifle. CONDITION: Excellent with 99% finish remaining. Wood is excellent. Bore is bright. Appears to be unfired from the factory. Fine mechanics. CALIBER: .44-40. FFL STATUS: Modern. S/N: JWF0003.
